Starter Kit Registers

Registers allow you to jump to a file or other location quickly. Use C-x r j followed by the letter of the register (i for init.el, s for this file) to jump to it.

You should add registers here for the files you edit most often. 

(dolist (r `((?i (file . ,(concat dotfiles-dir "init.el")))
             (?r (file . "~/src/emacs-starter-kit/starter-kit-registers.org"))
             (?s (file . "~/Documents/school/school.org"))
             (?e (file . "~/Documents/eric/eric.org"))
             (?g (file . "~/src/genprog/genprog.org"))
             (?h (file . "~/Documents/eric/homepage/eric-schulte.org"))
             (?t (file . "~/src/genprog/lp-genprog/quad/test.org"))
             (?l (file . "~/src/genprog/lp-genprog/modify.org"))
             (?b (file . "~/src/org-babel/org-babel.org"))
             (?z (file . "~/src/genprog/experiments/zitsers.org"))
             ;; classes
             (?A (file . "~/Documents/school/classes/cs691/cs691.org"))
             (?O (file . "~/Documents/school/classes/cs587/cs587.org"))
             (?R (file . "~/Documents/school/classes/cs591-rpc/cs591-rpc.org"))
             (?S (file . "~/Documents/school/classes/cs558/cs558.org"))
             ))
  (set-register (car r) (cadr r)))
